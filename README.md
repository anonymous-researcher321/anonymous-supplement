# Blinded supplementary material for DESRIST 2026 submission  
**"Design Knowledge for Ethical Use of AI in Emergency Medical Dispatch Systems"**

## Supplementary Materials

- **supplement_01_concept_matrix.pdf**  
  Concept matrix used to structure and synthesize the reviewed literature according to key ethical principles.

- **supplement_02_frequency_table.pdf**  
  Frequency table summarizing the distribution of ethical principles across the analyzed literature corpus.

- **supplement_03_interview_guide.pdf**  
  Interview guide containing the questions used for interviews with experts and lay participants.

- **supplement_04_gioia_visualization.pdf**  
  Visualization of the Gioia coding structure showing the abstraction from first-order concepts to second-order themes and aggregated dimensions.
